No matter what has happened to fair Atlantis, the simple truth of the matter is that you must become self-reliant.
@PHere in Spios, there seems to be trouble afoot.  The Mayans, who have always been docile, eager recipients of Atlantean knowledge and goods, seem changed, somehow. When asked to reveal the topic of their quiet, furtive conversation, they claim to be speaking about 'Nothing special.'
